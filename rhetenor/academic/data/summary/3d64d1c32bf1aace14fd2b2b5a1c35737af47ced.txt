In the example, Soybeans were a 13.9% better deal than Coffee.  
There is a direct but not constant relationship between margin requirement, volatility, and directional movement.  
The CSI equation continuously analyzes margin, volatility, and directional movement to identify the most advantageous situations.  
Margin requirements lag market action; they are slow to increase and slow to decrease.  
The Commodity Selection Index enables traders to exploit the lag in margin requirements to achieve the best return on invested capital.
