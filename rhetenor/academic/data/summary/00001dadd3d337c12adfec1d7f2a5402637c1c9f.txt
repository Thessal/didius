The first-ever trading in futures on the VIX Index began on March 26, 2004 on the CBOE Futures Exchange.  
VIX options were launched in February 2006.  
The VIX reached an intraday high of 89.53 on October 24, 2008.  
The VIX closed at a record 80.74 on November 21, 2008.  
On February 5, 2018, the VIX closed at 37.32, up 103.99% from the previous close.  
On March 9, 2020, the VIX hit 62.12, the highest level since the 2008 financial crisis.
